About Us

Nisha had always dreamt of opening a pudding shop. Thanks to you and other pudding lovers, her dream has come true.

Our pudding is made fresh daily in our secret pudding laboratory located beneath the streets of lovely Greenwich Village. We take great pride in concocting our delectable desserts from high quality, fair trade and organic ingredients, not that you expected anything less. Our grass-fed cows provide the signature creamy and rich taste that you have come to crave. No dyes, no preservatives - just natural, handcrafted pudding.
